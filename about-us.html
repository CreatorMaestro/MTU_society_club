About Us

What is the MTU Tetris Club?

Tetris University Club is a new opportunity for students.

Not only to learn how to play Tetris, but also to learn how to make the right decisions quickly and correctly, etc.

Our main goal is to make the university and students a single team as well as to raise the university in ranking and respect.

Benefits of the Club


  1. University tournaments every month

  2. Improve your math mind

  3. Imrove your logic mind

  4. Improve your communication skills

Most frequently questions

Do I need to pay something?

No, you don't need to pay, all it's free. You need just to be communicative and friendly.

Do I need to take a place in tournament?

It's not necessary to do this, but if you want to take participation, it's always welcome in the club.

What if my schedule isn't inconvenient?

You can tell us and we will manage schedule for club. Our target it's to do everything comfortable for everyone

Are there any rules in the club?

Yes, it is. We have only 2 rules.
1. Respect each other
2. Be yourself and have fun.

Will be another competition in the future?

Of course, we have competition every month among groups and in the future the best players will introduce our university among other universities.
